Steam Summer Sale Is Finally here

Steam Summer Sale Is Here, Prepare Your Wallets!



Yes that's right the the wait is over as Steam summer sale is finally here starting off strong with amazing deals already being posted tempting gamers all around the world to go out on the street and shake a cup just so we can afford them all. This years deals are starting off with great games like the GTA franchise ranging from 25% to 75% off, the Xcom Franchise for 50% to 83% off, Hotline Miami and Don't Starve Together for 10 euro and even Valves complete pack containing all of Valves games worth over 90 euro for just 23 euro.

Steam Summer Sale Event 2015

As you may or may not already know steam holds special events during its major sales, this is to keep customers interested and getting them more involved with the sale itself to hopefully generate more sales. This years event is the Monster Summer Game, so the way this event works is you play this game called Monster, complete milestones within the game and craft badges.

Monster is a point and click game where you basically click on the monsters and space ships to attack them, as you move the levels you can increase your attack power, armour and unlock abilities. To unlock additional abilities you must craft and level up your summer sale badge with summer sale cards your earn by playing the game, buying games on steam, crafting badges from other games and buying or trading them through the steam market.

How does this effect the summer sale I hear you ask, well every 24 hours the community is given certain milestones to complete within the game if the community can complete these milestone then Steam releases "special game discounts for everyone" which will be unveiled at 10am every morning. As this is the first day of the sale we still have no idea if these discounts will be worth all the time and effort but I trust it will be worth it because when it comes to a good sale that is one area steam never fails in.
